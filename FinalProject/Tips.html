Tips Before Bed

  1. Try to stay off any technology at least an hour before you sleep- this helps reduce blue light exposure.
  2. Set a sleep schedule- remain consistent with the times you go to bed and wake up as best as possible.
  3. Stretch or do yoga to reduce morning striffness or pain
  4. Only get in bed until you're ready to sleep
  5. Try to avoid eat big meals or food high in sugar before bed.

Environment

  1. Make sure the room is dark
  2. Reduce the tempature in your bedroom-65 degrees is the best temperature for sleeping
  3. Reduces the noise-if this is not possible use white noise to drowned out the sound
  4. Make sure your sleep environment is clean
  5. Make sure you feel safe-making sure your brain is not on high alter
